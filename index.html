The MEE8 Journey

In 2023, MEE8 emerged as a revolutionary Discord bot, built on the foundation of accessibility and premium features for all. Powered by the Pogy framework, it served over 1,000 members with advanced moderation tools and premium-quality features completely free of charge. Under the visionary leadership of Whelen, MEE8 proved that exceptional Discord bot functionality should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial means.

A Message from Whelen

While MEE8's services have been discontinued, its spirit of innovation and accessibility lives on. The decision to pause development stems from time constraints and our commitment to delivering nothing short of excellence. This isn't goodbye forever - it's a pause for reflection and improvement. When the time is right, MEE8 may return, stronger and better equipped to serve the Discord community. Thank you for being part of this journey, and stay tuned for what the future might hold.
